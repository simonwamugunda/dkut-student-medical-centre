Medical Centre Services

The Medical Centre services are available to all students, staff, and their dependents.

Our Services Include:

  • Out-patient services (Currently)
  • Laboratory services
  • Round-the-clock Ambulance services for cases requiring urgent medical referrals to other Medical Institutions.

For Staff / Dependants:

We have a memorandum of understanding with the following Institutions:

  • Mt. Kenya Hospital, Nyeri
  • Consolata Hospital, Nyeri
  • Outspan Hospital, Nyeri
  • Gertrude Hospital, Nairobi (and branches)

These are used for referrals in cases requiring specialized Diagnostic or Therapeutic services not available at our Medical Centre. The same Institutions are also used for admissions.

Arrangements are underway to bring other Institutions on board.
